Meet Dr. Roeder

Confidence, compassion, and surgical excellence.

Rosiane A. Roeder, MD, MBA, MPH, FACS

Dr. Roeder is a board-certified plastic surgeon with a passion for helping patients feel more confident through personalized, natural-looking results.

With advanced training in plastic and reconstructive surgery and years of experience managing complex surgical cases, she brings a refined skill set and an artistic eye to every procedure.

Known for empathy, thoughtfulness, and precision, Dr. Roeder takes time to understand your goals, concerns, and lifestyle. Her approach is collaborative and empowering.
